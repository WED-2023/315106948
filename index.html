What are the main differences between Web1, Web2, and Web3?

- Web1 (1990s-2000s): Static, read-only websites.
  Example: Simple blogs or news pages.
- Web2 (2000s-Present): Interactive, user-generated content.
  Example: Social media like Facebook.
- Web3 (Emerging): Decentralized, AI-driven, blockchain-based internet.
  Example: Cryptocurrency platforms.

How would a search query for "Side Effects of Medication" look in Web1 vs Web3?

- Web1: A simple article listing side effects from one source.
- Web3: AI-driven search pulling personalized, verified medical data from multiple sources with blockchain security.
